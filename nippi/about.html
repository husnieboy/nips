Welcome to 'Nippi.com'

As humans, we are all bound to experience "Culture shock" and it takes some time to get used to our new environment. Learning to accept the Japanese society and culture into your daily life can be a rich and rewarding experience, but at the same time it can also be challenging. Considering the fact that you have to adjust in unfamiliar surroundings and adapt to Japan’s social and cultural environment is not easy as it seems. Either permanently or temporarily – you need to immerse yourself in the local culture and let go of stereotypes and false impressions.

Nippi.com is well aware of the challenges you face, that’s why this is a great place to gather information about life in Japan and even entertainment to help you ease the process of making the transition from living like an expat to a more Japanese life-style.

Our Mission

Nippi.com's mission is to widely disseminate information to every Filipino living and/or working in Japan, particularly informations that affect us - in one way or another.

Just like the weather, any information of "something" can change or will change in due time so Nippi.com makes it a point that ALL THE INFORMATIONS are reasonably up-to-date. If you find something that is not, kindly CONTACT us.
