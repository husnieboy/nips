Having a bank account is a convenient way to pay utility bills, receive your salary through bank transfer from your company and even pay taxes.

Anyone who holds a RESIDENCE CARD issued by the Government of Japan can open a bank account and in some case, a DRIVER'S LICENSE is accepted in some banks.
